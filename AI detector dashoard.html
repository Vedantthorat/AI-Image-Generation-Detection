How Detection Works

Six forensic techniques analysed simultaneously for maximum accuracy

01
🖼
Preprocessing
Image is resized to 32×32, normalised to [0,1] and passed through the augmentation pipeline before CNN inference.
02
🧠
CNN Feature Extraction
Three Conv2D blocks with BatchNorm + MaxPool extract hierarchical visual features — textures, edges, semantic patterns.
03
📊
Softmax Classifier
The Dense output head produces a two-class probability vector. The AI score is confidence assigned to the "AI-generated" class.
04
📈
Histogram Analysis
AI images have unnaturally smooth RGB histograms with fewer high-frequency spikes than real photographs from camera sensors.
05
🌊
Frequency Fingerprint
GAN and diffusion models leave periodic artefacts in frequency space — visible as bright off-centre spots in the frequency map.
06
⚡
In-Browser TF.js
Keras model exported via tensorflowjs_converter runs entirely in the browser. No uploads, no server — full privacy guaranteed.
